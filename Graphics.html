Graphics Quality

R6' graphics can be manipulated and changed in many ways. that makes them suitable for any kind of gaming 'machine'. The graphics are well optimized and can run even on "Intel HD Graphics" GPU. Video card drivers play a crucial part in the rendering of the game. Nvidia's Geforce Experiance allows the player a high variety of Graphic Customisation tools.


Customisation Menu

The Display and Graphics settings must be paid a good chunk of attention because if you aren't cautious enough you may place yourself in a competitive disadvantage simply because some of the screen effects that the game offers have impact on your gameplay when pumped up to max. We suggest turning off all the effects including anti aliasing so you can have better chances of winning games. :D
